# Backend Business Rules

## Leave
- Sick leave: 5 per year
- Casual leave: 5 per year
- Earned leave: 1.5/month (usable after 1 year)
- Leave validation happens server-side

## Timesheets
- Max 8 hours/day
- Weekly submission mandatory
- Only assigned projects allowed
- Rejected timesheets must be corrected

## Employees
- Email & employee ID must be unique
- Inactive employees cannot submit data
